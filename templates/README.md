# Templates

This directory contains analysis templates for applying safety and security standards to autonomous driving systems.

---

## Available Templates

### HARA_Template.md
**Hazard Analysis and Risk Assessment (ISO 26262)**

Use this template to:
- Identify hazards in autonomous driving systems
- Classify severity, exposure, and controllability
- Determine ASIL levels for safety requirements
- Define safety goals and requirements

### SOTIF_Analysis_Template.md
**Safety of Intended Functionality Analysis (ISO 21448)**

Use this template to:
- Identify performance limitations and triggering conditions
- Classify scenarios using the 4-quadrant model
- Analyze unknown unsafe scenarios
- Define validation strategies for AI perception systems

### TARA_Template.md
**Threat Analysis and Risk Assessment (ISO/SAE 21434)**

Use this template to:
- Identify cybersecurity threats to autonomous vehicles
- Assess attack feasibility and impact
- Define security requirements
- Plan mitigation strategies

---

## How to Use

1. Copy the relevant template for your analysis
2. Fill in the sections specific to your system/component
3. Review with safety/security experts
4. Integrate findings into your development process

These templates are meant as starting points. Adapt them based on your specific project requirements and organizational processes.

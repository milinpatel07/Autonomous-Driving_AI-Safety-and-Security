# Module 07: Regulation and Legal Framework

How do regulations govern autonomous vehicle development and deployment?

This module covers the regulatory landscape, legal frameworks, and certification requirements for autonomous vehicles across different regions.

---

## What's in This Module

**Topics covered:**
- International regulatory frameworks
- Regional regulations (EU, US, China, Japan)
- Certification and homologation processes
- Liability frameworks for autonomous vehicles
- Insurance and risk allocation
- Ethical guidelines and frameworks

---

## Why This Matters

**Regulation determines market access:**
- Type approval required for vehicle deployment
- Different requirements across regions
- Evolving regulations as technology advances
- Legal liability affects system design choices

**Key regulatory bodies:**
- UNECE WP.29 (UN regulations)
- NHTSA (United States)
- European Commission (EU)
- SAE International (standards)

**Current challenges:**
- Regulations lag behind technology
- Inconsistent requirements across jurisdictions
- Unclear liability frameworks for AI decisions
- Balance between innovation and safety
